About Me

Based in Salzburg, Austria, I work at the intersection of physical craftsmanship and digital design. My background in modelmaking informs everything I create — from mechanical components to generative art.

With years of experience in precision modeling, 3D design, and material studies, I bring ideas to life through a combination of traditional techniques and modern technology.

When I'm not designing, you'll find me on bicycles, motorbicycles, or climbing in the Austrian Alps.
